calibration to pick a fair reduced step budget, then trains a fresh baseline plus three single-knob
variants (no weight-tying, no positional embeddings, depth cut 6→3 layers) through the UNTOUCHED
`train()` harness at identical seed/data/LR/budget, scores each with the deterministic `perplexity()`
from Plan 01, and emits a committed comparison table.

cohort that isolates ONE design choice per variant. The no-weight-tying ablation deliberately
exercises the real Phase-4 weight-tying seam (the flag Plan 02 added), so the ablation story and
the from-scratch implementation reinforce each other.

the comparison table committed under the tracked `results/` path.
